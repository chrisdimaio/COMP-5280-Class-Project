About Us

Our Mission Statement:

We wanted to create an easy to use website to assist people trying to learn English.



How To Use This Website:

Have you come across a word and can't figure out its meaning?

No problem!

When you search a word in this website, you will be given first a formal definition from a dictionary. But, we also allow our users to enter their own definition of words. That means that you learn from fellow English Learners! We also encourage you to contribute to our database. You can either enter your own definitions or upvote an user definition.
